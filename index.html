Mindfulness?

What is mindfulness?

Mindfulness means paying attention to the present moment. You notice your thoughts, feelings, and surroundings without judging them. It helps you feel calm, focused, and more aware.

Mindfulness?

Why it's important?

Mindfulness is important because it helps you stay calm, focused, and aware of the present. It can lower stress, improve mental health, and help you make better choices. You feel more relaxed and in control of your thoughts.
